Privacy Policy for SafeTNet User App

Effective date: March 18, 2026

SafeTNet User App ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the SafeTNet User App.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account information such as your name, phone number, email address, and profile details.
  • Location data, including precise and background location, for live tracking, geofencing, SOS, and safety features.
  • Emergency contact information that you add in the app.
  • Camera, microphone, photo, audio, and media data when you capture or upload evidence, images, or recordings.
  • Device and app information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash data, and diagnostic data.
  • Notification tokens and related technical data used to deliver alerts and notifications.
  • Subscription and purchase status information related to Google Play billing.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Provide safety features such as SOS alerts, live location sharing, and geofence monitoring.
  • Allow you to capture, upload, and share images, audio, and other safety evidence.
  • Send notifications, alerts, and service messages.
  • Enable premium subscription features and verify Google Play purchases.
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to valid legal requests.

3. Permissions Used by the App

The app may request access to the following permissions when needed for core functionality:

  • Camera to capture photos or evidence.
  • Microphone to record audio where supported by app features.
  • Location to provide live location, geofencing, and emergency assistance features.
  • Phone and SMS to support emergency calling and messaging features initiated by the user.
  • Storage or media access to attach, save, or read selected media files.
  • Notifications to deliver alerts and updates.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers and infrastructure partners that help us operate the app.
  • Google Play for subscription billing and purchase verification.
  • Firebase or related Google services for notifications and app functionality.
  • Law enforcement or government authorities where required by law or necessary to protect safety, rights, or property.
  • Emergency contacts or designated recipients when you trigger sharing or SOS-related features.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide the app, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and support safety-related functionality.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to help protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

SafeTNet User App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Choices

You may:

  • Deny certain permissions, though some app features may not work properly.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data where applicable.

9. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google Play and Firebase. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.
